Calogero

One of the greatest singers ever

Here's a bit of Calogero's life, helping the good cause :

  • 1971 - Born in Echirolles, Isère
  • 2002 - Participate in the song "Y a pas un homme qui soit né pour ça" with Florent Pagny and Pascal Obispo for sidaction (Association fighting against AIDS)
  • 2006 - Participate in the single "douce France" leaded by Marc Lavoine to fight discrimination and help youth getting a job
  • 2010 - Sings in the concert "300 jours déjà" in solidarity for Hervé Ghesquière and Stéphane Taponier
  • 2014 - Participate in the show dedicated to Grégory Lemarchai to help researches on mucoviscidovis
  • 2017 - Sings "Les feux d'artifice" in tribute to people killed in an attack in Nice
  • 2018 - Plays for the fundation GoodPlanet
  • 2020 - Release the song "On fait comme si" for the hospitals
